The Internet + WWW

The Internet — like the expansion of books published after Gutenberg’s invention — provides a means by which the public can discover the lies, fraud, and corruption practiced by the state against the rest of us. It is the corporate-state that seeks the protection of the censorship inherent in these efforts to make the Internet useful to political interests. This is a major reason why the mainstream media has been losing viewers and readers to the Internet, a fact that makes CNN’s plea as disingenuous as listening to Detroit auto-makers criticizing their Japanese competitors!
