Book: June 29, 1999

This is an old favorite.  I first heard about it on Reading Rainbow.  David Wiesner tells a story through pictures of fruits and vegetables dropping down from the sky.  Of course they are all the size of skyscrapers. 
  • Cucumbers circle Kalamazoo. 
  • Lima beans loom over Levittown.
No one gets seriously hurt and it has a pretty funny ending.  For $6, pick up a copy for your child @ Amazon.com.  You can also see more pictures @ the publisher's website.
